🗺️ How to earn miles

Every confirmed SmartSense LIVE registration you land earns 1 mile. The CSM with the most total miles at each checkpoint wins that month's prize.

There are also Nitro Boosts — bonus miles for key moments:

  • +2 First registration you land each month
  • +2 Enterprise or strategic account registers
  • +3 Customer brings 3 or more attendees
  • +2 An at-risk account registers

👥 Crew challenge

You compete on two tracks simultaneously — as an individual and as part of your crew. Three crews of 6 CSMs compete each month. The crew with the most combined miles wins coffee gift cards for the entire crew.

Crews are balanced by tenure, account size, and book diversity.

🏆 Season champion — LIVE Legend

The CSM with the highest total miles across all four months (May–August) wins the grand prize: a premium experience gift and recognition on stage at SmartSense LIVE in front of every customer in the room.

Season champion determined September 8. Announced at LIVE.

Leaderboard updates every Monday. Report registrations in #race-to-live on Slack.
